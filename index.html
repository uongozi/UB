About UBA - The Inaugural Award Show

The UBA-Uongozi Bora Awards is an inaugural Award Show recognizing Leadership Excellence In Government, Corporate & NGO with the spotlight being on East Africa and Central Africa. We celebrate proven leaders driving positive change and sustainable impact across these foundational sectors based on our five pillars:

✓ INTEGRITY   ✓ ACCOUNTABILITY   ✓ RELIABILITY   ✓ ACCEPTABILITY   ✓ VIABILITY

Our Event Location: Woodley Estate, Nairobi Area, Kenya.
